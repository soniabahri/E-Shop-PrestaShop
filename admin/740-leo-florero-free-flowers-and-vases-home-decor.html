About the Theme

 

Leo Florero is best free Prestashop 1.7 theme for flower vase, glass vase, decorative vases, home decor store. This responsive theme has an attractive look thanks to its great color combination, eye-catchy images, flexible but logical layout to display and outstand your beautiful products in an effective way.  

Leo Florero Free with remarkable logo and powerful mega menu at the top center together with a full-width slider makes your eCommerce store a murderer of beauty lovers right at the first time they access your store. You can show vases for sale or beautiful glass flower vases on slideshow or banners to your clients. It is also integrated with wonderful features like Swipe, Owl Carousel, etc to bring the whole theme a smooth sense. Moreover, Leo Florero is particularly suited for promotions events by the support of Time Countdown function.

Built on the best Prestashop Framework and Ap Pagebuilder module, this vase Prestashop template allows you to display your products or items in the smart way as it supports 7+ layouts for product detail page and 2+ layouts for blog page. Leo Florero Free is user-friendly, you can customize and manage your site in the powerful admin panel with ease just by some Drag n Drop manipulations.

Plus, Leo Florero Free comes with Leo Feature module with more user-utilities like Preview, Add to Wishlist, Add to Cart, Add to Compare.
